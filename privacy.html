Privacy Policy for Plain Site

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Plain Site (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Plain Site mobile application (the “App”).

By using Plain Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use Plain Site, you may choose to provide:

  • Personal Information: Names, phone numbers, addresses, dates of birth, emergency contacts
  • Health-Related Information: Medical or intake information (if you choose to store it)
  • Documents: Photos of IDs, insurance cards, vaccination records, and other files
  • Custom Data: Any additional information you manually enter

⚠️ Important:
You control what information you provide. Plain Site does not require specific data to function.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

We may collect limited technical data:

  • Usage Data: Feature usage and interactions
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version
  • Error Logs: Crash reports and diagnostics

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT:

  • Access contacts, camera, or microphone without permission
  • Track your location
  • Collect browsing history
  • Sell your data
  • Share your data with advertisers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data only to:

  • Provide app functionality
  • Enable optional cloud sync
  • Improve performance and fix bugs
  • Provide support
  • Maintain security

We do NOT sell your personal data.

4. How We Store Your Information

4.1 Local Storage (Default)

By default, your data:

  • Is stored locally on your device
  • Does not leave your device
  • Works offline

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync:

  • Data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Files are stored in Firebase Storage
  • Data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Data is encrypted at rest

You can delete your cloud data at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement:

  • Encryption in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Firebase security rules
  • Secure authentication
  • Local-first architecture

⚠️ However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

Local Data

  • Stored until you delete it or uninstall the app

Cloud Data

  • Stored until you delete your account
  • Deleted within ~30 days after deletion request

7. Your Rights and Controls

Deletion

Cloud Control
